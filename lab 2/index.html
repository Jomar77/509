Kongregate: 2010

Why choose this?

I chose this website as I was growing up, this would be my go to for playing games. Ranging from the kingdom rush series to last stand series, I made a lot of memories here. This website was launched on 2006 by Emily and Jim Greer. The purpose of the platform was for anyone to upload games and find potential audiences.

Analysis of the website:2010

2010 was a time where Kongregate really picked up a lot of gamers around the world. Albeit me starting gaming at 2011, I think 2010 deserve a remark in regards to its design and popularity. Here we can see the "OG" design that would be the face of Kongregate for many years. This design is simple and User friendly. One example of showcasing such trait would be the feature games in upon opening the website. The picture above won't show it but it has an animation that would showcase trending games.

Here we can see some games that have been seperated by category for gamers to enjoy. Back then, indie games were the trend and as such, they usually take up a category menu. As for the technical side of the website, each of the category box is inside a description list tag < dl > which is then contained inside a div. we can also see that there is a news section which is also inside a div. Futhermore, the text inside is in a <dd> tag.
